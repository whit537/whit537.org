title = "Reactions to Jesus" published = "2008-12-08T13:22:00.004-05:00" updated = "2011-04-10T09:52:45.030-04:00" ^L ^L {% extends "base.html" %} {% block content %} I can think of 6 reactions to Jesus:
  1. Jesus was evil.
  2. Jesus was a nice fairy tale.
  3. Jesus was a wise man.
  4. Jesus was a prophet from God.
  5. Jesus was a divine being.
  6. Jesus was the Second Person of the Trinity.
Am I missing any?

Christian Minimalism exists to subsume all of these positions under the heading of "Christianity." Yes, I can imagine someone who thinks that Jesus was evil but considers themselves Christian. And therefore that person exists. And that person is in the same religion that I am in, if they want to be.

I am willing to be identified with any human.

I am willing for any human to identify me with any other human.
