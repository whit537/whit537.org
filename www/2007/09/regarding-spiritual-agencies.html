Some easily ascribe these coincidences to an unseen personal agency. But perhaps the true intentionality in the Universe is not seen in the micro-patterns that are within our narrow, narrow field of vision—and our largest view is always infinitely small; and yet indeed we might properly enjoy these "snippets of meaning" when we "pass through them" (so to speak); and in fact, as agents ourselves we may try to effect such orderings. Rather, the true "meaning" of the Universe—the order in the chaos, the signal in the noise, the information in the transmission, the pattern in the data—is perhaps only seen when the whole seemingly random complex messiness of the entire infinite π-line is gathered up into the stunningly, beautifully simple ratio: circumference to diameter.

Then again, Jack tells some really crazy demon stories. So I dunno.
